About Us

Plant Palette is a vegetarian restaurant dedicated to making a variety of delicious dishes without compromising on taste. Using fresh, organic, and locally grown ingredients, we can promise that Plant Palette will become a restaurant you’ll want to return to.

Our Staff

John Cook

Head Chef

Cooking has always been my passion, and one of my favourite aspects of that is finding new and creative ways to add a healthy and eco friendly twist to new dishes. One thing I really appreciate about working at Plant Palette is that I’m encouraged to come up with menus that won't be found at other vegetarian restaurants.

Marie Smith

Restaurant Owner

I've always been a major foodie, but I want to pay attention to more than just taste. Plant Palette was my solution to an issue I noticed in Ottawa's restaurant scene: there were very few vegetarian restaurants in the city, and that meant next to no variety. When I first opened the restaurant, I decided that it would always offer something new, so our guests could never say they got tired of eating here.

Walter Green

Waiter

Environmental consciousness and community outreach are important to me, so I really wanted to find work alongside like minded people. Plant Palette seemed like the perfect opportunity for me, and I’m glad to say that working here has been an absolute blast.

FAQ

Ingredient Sourcing

Almost all of our ingredients come from local, organic farms. Because of this, much of our menu changes throughout the year according to what’s in season. Please note that we do need to have some of our ingredients brought from further away, as we try to keep a small selection of dishes available year-round.

Diet Accommodations

We will try to accommodate any dietary restrictions our diners have, and several common ones are indicated on any of our dishes that contain them. Furthermore, if requested, we will try to make your dish without any ingredients that may be problematic for you.

Catering

Our catering services are available for pick-up or delivery, and intended for orders of 7 servings or larger. Please place your order at lease 24 hours in advance when ordering 15 servings or less, or 48 hours in advance when ordering more than 15 servings.

Reservations

We offer reservations to ensure diners can have the table they need, when they need it. Please book large group reservations at least 24 hours in advance, and group reservations at least 7 days in advance. Should you need to cancel a reservation, if it is for a large group, please give at least 2 hours notice, and if it is an event, give at least 48 hours notice.

Other Information

Our location in downtown Ottawa has street parking available, and is wheelchair-accessible. We maintain a no tipping required policy, meaning that diners are not expected to tip with their meals, as the staff are paid more than restaurants that expect tips. However, this does mean our prices are slightly higher than other establishments.
